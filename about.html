About Me

I’m currently enrolled as a UX Designer apprentice in Bloc’s Designer Track Program. My experience includes roles as a User Experience Designer and Researcher.

After several years of working in Customer Service, I wanted to shift towards a career that combined my strengths in working with end users of a product or service, along with my love of technology. I spent six months attending various career workshops on web design and development to help me visualize the life I wanted to create. I came to realize that I’ve always been most passionate about researching how things can be made easier and more user-friendly. This passion led me to a career in User Experience design.

I hold a Bachelor’s Degree in Communication Arts from Mount Saint Mary College.
